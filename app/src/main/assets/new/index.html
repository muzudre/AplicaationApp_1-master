About Limkokwingclose

Limkokwing University of Creative Technology (referred to as LUCT, LKW or just Limkokwing) is a private international university with a presence across Africa, Europe and Asia. With its main campus in Malaysia, the university has over 30,000 students from more than 150 countries, studying in its 12 other campuses in Botswana, Cambodia, China, Indonesia, Lesotho, Sierra Leone, Swaziland and the United Kingdom accredited by Accreditation Service for International Colleges and Universities (ASIC).

About Limkokwing Londonclose

Limkokwing London offers opportunities for students from our other campuses to study with us on their substantive courses (subject to numbers) for one semester or one academic year as part of a study abroad arrangement. This is in addition to our one month Global University Campus programme. Students from other universities can join our Global University Campus programme and study with us for one semester as part of an agreed collaborative study abroad arrangement.
